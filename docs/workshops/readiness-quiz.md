# Supersuit Up Readiness Quiz

*Eight questions to figure out where you are and what your best next step is. Takes two minutes.*

---

The [Supersuit Up workshop](/docs/workshops/supersuit-up) is a hands-on session where you build a Personal Agentic OS from scratch. It is not for everyone. Some people will get massive value from it. Some already have what it teaches. Some are not ready yet and would benefit more from a different starting point.

This quiz helps you figure out which category you are in so you do not waste your time or anyone else's.

Answer each question honestly. Add up your score at the end.

---

## The Questions

### 1. How do you feel about AI right now?

- **(A)** I think AI is overhyped, dangerous, or something I want to avoid. (0 points)
- **(B)** I am curious but have not done much with it beyond trying ChatGPT a few times. (2 points)
- **(C)** I use AI tools regularly and want to go deeper. (3 points)
- **(D)** I have a working AI system that I use daily for real work. (4 points)

### 2. Have you ever used a terminal (command line)?

- **(A)** I do not know what that is. (1 point)
- **(B)** I have seen one but never used it myself. (2 points)
- **(C)** I have used it a few times (installing software, running commands). (3 points)
- **(D)** I am comfortable in the terminal and use it regularly. (4 points)

### 3. Do you have a system for organizing your goals, projects, and relationships?

- **(A)** No, it is all in my head. (2 points)
- **(B)** I use notes apps, spreadsheets, or a CRM but nothing AI-connected. (3 points)
- **(C)** I have markdown files or a structured folder system that an AI tool can read. (4 points)
- **(D)** I have a Personal Agentic OS with a context lake, skill files, and daily brain dumps. (5 points)

### 4. What is your relationship with AI tools like Claude Code, Cursor, or HERMES?

- **(A)** I have never heard of these. (2 points)
- **(B)** I have heard of them but never tried them. (2 points)
- **(C)** I have tried one but did not stick with it or could not get it set up. (3 points)
- **(D)** I use one of these daily with my own instruction files and context. (5 points)

### 5. When someone tells you AI can help you with your work, what is your first reaction?

- **(A)** Skepticism or resistance. "That is not how I do things." (0 points)
- **(B)** Interest, but I do not know where to start. (2 points)
- **(C)** Excitement. I have been looking for the right way to set this up. (3 points)
- **(D)** "I already do this. Show me what is next." (4 points)

### 6. Are you willing to speak out loud to your computer (voice-to-text)?

- **(A)** Absolutely not. That feels weird. (0 points)
- **(B)** I would try it if someone showed me how. (2 points)
- **(C)** I already use voice notes or dictation sometimes. (3 points)
- **(D)** Voice-to-text is my primary input method. (4 points)

### 7. Do you have a laptop you can bring that is less than 5 years old?

- **(A)** No, my computer is very old or I do not have one. (0 points)
- **(B)** Yes, but it is slow and I am not sure it can handle new software. (1 point)
- **(C)** Yes, it is reasonably modern and works fine. (3 points)
- **(D)** Yes, it is a recent machine with plenty of storage and RAM. (3 points)

### 8. What do you want to get out of AI?

- **(A)** I do not want anything from AI. I am here because someone told me to be. (0 points)
- **(B)** I want to save time and automate repetitive tasks. (2 points)
- **(C)** I want an AI thinking partner that knows my full context and helps me make better decisions. (3 points)
- **(D)** I want to build systems that compound and get smarter every day I use them. (3 points)

---

## Your Score

Add up your points and find your result.

### 0-8 points: Not Ready Yet

You are either resistant to AI or missing some of the [minimum viable infrastructure](/docs/concepts/minimum-viable-infrastructure) to participate. That is okay. The workshop would frustrate you right now and slow down the people around you.

**Your next step:** Come to an [Applied AI Live event](https://appliedaisociety.org) or watch someone use these tools for five minutes. That is usually all it takes. Once you have had [the encounter](/docs/concepts/the-encounter), everything clicks. Read [Your Two Futures](/docs/philosophy/your-two-futures) to understand what is at stake. When the curiosity hits, come back and retake this quiz.

### 9-18 points: Perfect Fit

You are curious, open, and ready to build. You have enough technical comfort to follow instructions and enough motivation to push through the setup. This is exactly who the workshop is designed for.

**Your next step:** [Sign up for a Supersuit Up workshop](/docs/workshops/supersuit-up). You will walk out with a working Personal Agentic OS on your laptop. Having an experienced practitioner in the room will save you hours of debugging and get you to the "aha" moment faster than going solo.

### 19-25 points: Partially Onboarded

You are already using AI tools and may have a loose system going. The workshop would fill gaps and formalize what you have, but you could also work through the [written guide](/docs/workshops/supersuit-up) on your own.

**Your next step:** If you want the structure and the in-person help, attend the workshop. You will still get value, especially from the interview exercises and the brain dump practice. If you prefer self-paced, the guide has everything you need. Either way, make sure you have a USER.md, a context lake with relationship files, and at least one skill file. If you are missing any of those, the workshop will fill the gap fast.

### 26-35 points: Already Suited Up

You have a working Personal Agentic OS. You do not need the workshop. You are the kind of person who should be helping run one.

**Your next step:** If you want to help others suit up, read [Training the Workshop](/docs/playbooks/practitioner/training-the-workshop) and reach out on [Discord](https://discord.gg/K7uWJBMFaN). We need practitioners who can run these sessions in their cities. The [Raise the Floor](/docs/concepts/raise-the-floor) principle: your breakthrough should become everyone's baseline. Help us raise it.

---

*Take this quiz honestly. There is no shame in any result. The only wrong answer is pretending you are somewhere you are not.*
