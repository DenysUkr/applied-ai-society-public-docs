## The Future Economy Is the Applied Economy

Most people still think of AI as something that happens in data centers or research labs. But the real story is playing out in businesses, classrooms, and organizations across every sector. The applied economy is not coming. It is here.

And it deserves a week dedicated to helping people understand how to upskill into it.

## What to Expect

- **Thought leadership** from the people actually building in applied AI right now. Not theorists. Practitioners.
- **The best minds gathered in one place.** Leaders, founders, chapter organizers, and the practitioners who are making money in this economy today.
- **Austin, Texas.** The applied AI capital of the world. This is where the movement is being built.
- **A national expansion in 2027.** What starts in Austin will spread to cities across the country.
